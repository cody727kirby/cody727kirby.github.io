Supporting Act Performances for Etta James at the Apollo

Date range confirmed in Apollo ad (From Chris Cadman’s “Michael Jackson the Maestro”) (“Starts Friday May 24”) and Chris Cadman (“24th – 30th May, 1968”)

 

Chris Cadman, author, “Michael Jackson the Maestro”

It would be during this time that The Jackson 5 was both discovered by Gladys Knight and Bobby Taylor who was with the Vancouver’s at the time. Gladys Knight & the Pips had signed with Motown in 1966 and were the first to contact Motown about the young group. But it was Bobby Taylor who took the initiative when he asked Suzanne De Passé, to take a look at them and tell Berry Gordy about their exciting potential. The rest of course is history.
