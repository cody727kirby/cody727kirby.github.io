Supporting Act for The Supremes

Date confirmed on the official website for Michael Jackson (archived) (“August 16, 1969”)

 

Michael Jackson official website (August 16, 2018) (archived)

On August 16, 1969, the Jackson 5 supported Diana Ross & The Supremes at the Los Angeles Forum, as Diana helped introduce the group to the world.
