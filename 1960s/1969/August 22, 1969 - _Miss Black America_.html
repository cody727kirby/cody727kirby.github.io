"Miss Black America"

Date confirmed on “TV-a-Go-Go: Rock on TV from American Bandstand to American Idol” (archived) (“August 22, 1969”)

 

Michael Jackson (From his 1988 book, Moonwalk)

Once “I Want You Back” came out, everyone at Motown prepared us for success. Diana loved it and presented us at a big-name Hollywood discotheque, where she had us playing in a comfortable party atmosphere like at Berry’s. Following directly on the heels of Diana’s event came an invitation to play at the “Miss Black America” telecast. Being on the show would enable us to give people a preview of our record and our show.
