For one week beginning Friday, June 20, the Apollo Theatre will feature a show starring the young black sound of America.

This sound will be created by young, vibrant performers projecting a completely contemporary feeling.

These stars are: Joe Simon, Five Stairsteps and Cubie, The Jackson Five and Johnny, the Lollipops and R.V. Hudman.
